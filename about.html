About BeamMaster AI

We are building the most accessible, beautiful, and computationally precise structural engineering platform — completely free for students, educators, and practicing engineers worldwide.

🎯

Our Mission

To democratize access to professional-grade engineering analysis tools. We believe every engineering student and professional deserves free, accurate computational tools — regardless of their location or financial resources.

⚡

Technical Excellence

All calculations use closed-form analytical solutions with mathematical superposition methods. Zero approximation errors, accurate to the last decimal — verified against established engineering references and Indian Standard codes.

🌍

Open & Accessible

BeamMaster AI runs entirely in your browser with no software installation required. It works on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices. No registration walls, no hidden fees — just engineering tools that work.

📐

Code Compliant

Our engineering calculators and educational articles reference IS 456, IS 10262, IS 1893, ACI 318, Eurocode, and NBC India standards. We bridge theoretical fundamentals with real-world code requirements.

What We Offer

A comprehensive engineering platform covering structural analysis, design education, and emerging technologies in civil engineering.

📊

Beam Analysis Suite

Interactive SFD, BMD, and deflection calculators supporting simply supported, cantilever, fixed, and overhanging beams with point loads, UDLs, and concentrated moments. Drag-and-drop load positioning with real-time animated diagrams.

📝

Engineering Articles

In-depth, original articles covering civil engineering, structural design, AI applications in engineering, geopolymer concrete, sustainable construction, and modern engineering software comparisons.

🤖

AI Engineering Assistant

Built-in AI assistant that explains structural concepts, reviews your active beam setup, and provides contextual engineering guidance based on Indian Standard and international codes.

📄

Professional Reports

Export your analysis results as formatted PDF reports or Excel CSV data — complete with reaction forces, peak values, and diagram visualizations ready for project documentation.
